# Team Roles and Responsibilities

## Review Team

| Role | Name | Affiliation | Responsibilities |
|---|---|---|---|
| Student 1 (R1) | Undergraduate Student 1 | NED UET | Independent screening, extraction |
| Student 2 (R2) | Undergraduate Student 2 | NED UET | Search strategy design, synthesis approach guidance  |

## Decision Authority

- **Inclusion/exclusion conflicts**: Discussed by R1 and R2; unresolved
  cases adjudicated by the discussion between R1 and R2.
- **Risk of bias disagreements**: Resolved through discussion.
## PRISMA 2020 alignment

This review follows PRISMA 2020 (Page et al., BMJ 2021) for reporting and
PRISMA-P 2015 (Moher et al., Syst Rev 2015) for the protocol.

## Communication

- Weekly team meeting: Saturday as per schedule
- Shared workspace: this Git repository
- Issue tracking: GitHub Issues, Private Discussion
