**Comment:** PCC framework appropriately translated to three Boolean
concept blocks. Approved.

### 2. Boolean and proximity operators
**Comment:** Boolean operators correctly used. No proximity operators
needed for this topic. Approved.

### 3. Subject headings (database-specific controlled vocabulary)

for "GraphRAG" (too new). IEEE Xplore index terms include "knowledge
representation" and "natural language processing." Author-supplied
keywords used in addition. Approved with note: rely on free-text since
controlled vocabularies lag the field.

### 4. Text word searching
**Comment:** Free-text search across title, abstract, and author keywords
appropriately covers terminology variation. Approved.

### 5. Spelling, syntax, line numbers
**Comment:** No spelling errors. Boolean syntax correct for each database.
Approved.

### 6. Limits and filters
**Comment:** Date range 2022-2026 appropriate; English-only and document
type filters justified in protocol. Approved.
